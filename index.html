Pseudoword Generator

What is it?

In short, a pseudoword generator can randomly create 'unique' or 'fake' words, based on patterns found in real english words. You can use this tool for creative writing purposes, as a password generator, or just for fun.

How to Use:

First 2 Letters:

These will be the first 2 'root' letters that will appear in the randomly generated words, and must be lowercase.

Count:

How many pseudowords to be generated, max of 100 words.

Propogation:

How likely it is for the word to be longer, so a greater value means longer words. Max propogation value of 100, and the generator will not generate words longer than 24 characters.

Randomness:

This is the 'weirdness' factor in what kind of letters will be used to make the pseudowords. A higher value means more obscure words, which are sometimes unpronounceable. Max randomness of 100.

Character biases:

Lets say you wanted to generate psuedowords that had the letters "p" and "h" in them. You can add character biases, which will multiply the chance of that specific character appearing the pseudowords. A bias multiplier less than 1 means that it has a less likely chance of appearing, and a value greater than 1 means that character has a higher chance of appearing in the pseudoword.
